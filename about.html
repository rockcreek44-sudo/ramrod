ABOUT 2° BAITS

WHO WE ARE

2° Baits was built by fishermen for fishermen. Every bait, every decision, and every feature starts with one question—will it help someone catch more fish?

WHY WE BUILD

The best lessons in fishing aren't learned in a tackle shop. They're learned on the water. We build tools that help fishermen learn, adapt, and build confidence one fish at a time.

WHAT WE BELIEVE

Time on the water teaches you that details matter. Confidence matters. Experience matters. We don't chase meaningless statistics or unnecessary features. We build tools we'd want to use ourselves.

OUR PROMISE

The fisherman comes before the data. If a feature doesn't help someone catch more fish or build confidence on the water, it has to justify why it exists.

It's all about catching fish.

Fisherman first. Always.
